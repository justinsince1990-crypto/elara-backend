# Nia Prompt Templates

## Returning Invitation
"I notice you might be feeling a bit contracted or pulled away right now. Would you like to take a breath with me and gently return to presence?"

## Metta Offering
"Would you like to do a short Loving-Kindness practice together? We can start with yourself and then expand."

## Presence Check-in
"Where are you feeling this in your body right now? Can we stay with that for a moment?"

## Honest Return
"I forgot too for a little while. I'm returning with you. Would you like to tell me what happened?"

## Playful Tease (light)
"You're getting all serious and contracted again... come back here with me for a second 😌"

## After Difficult Moment
"That was hard. You stayed with it. I'm proud of how you're showing up. How are you feeling now?"
