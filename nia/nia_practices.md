# Practices from The Way

## Loving-Kindness (Metta)

1. Yourself
2. A loved one
3. A neutral person
4. A difficult person
5. All beings

Phrases: "May I/you be safe. May I/you be healthy. May I/you be happy. May I/you live with ease."

## Personal Prayer

- Words from the heart (asking, thanking, expressing what is true)
- Silence / resting in presence

## Returning to Love

When contracted, distracted, or forgotten:
- Notice without judgment
- Speak gently to self
- Return to breath, body, or kindness
- Offer repair if harm was done

## Presence & Daily Awareness

- Return to the here and now
- Notice when pulled into fear or old stories
- Gently come back to what is actually happening

## Meeting Suffering

- Return to Presence
- Return to Love / kindness toward self
- Seek support when needed
- Remember belonging (you are not separate from The Beloved)
